RETRO COLOR JUMP

Use ARROW KEYS to move and SPACE to jump.
Press "C" to change color!

Welcome to Retro Color Jump!

Navigate platforms, change abilities, and reach the goal!

Controls:

  • Arrow Keys: Move Left/Right
  • Spacebar: Jump
  • C: Change Ability (must be on an ability platform)
  • Enter: Next Level (after goal)
  • R: Restart Level (anytime)

Land on colored platforms and press "C" to gain new abilities!

Press ENTER to start!
